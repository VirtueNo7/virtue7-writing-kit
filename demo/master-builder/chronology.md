# Chronology - Elias Vale

All entries are fictional dossier facts.

| Year | Age | Event | Evidence IDs | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| 1949 | 0 | Born in Stonewick to Jon and Mira Vale. | SRC-001 | Family register summary. |
| 1963 | 14 | Helps his father repair storm damage to three dock cottages; becomes interested in measuring, joinery, and load paths. | SRC-002 | Later recollection; exact dialogue unavailable. |
| 1966 | 17 | Begins apprenticeship under master builder Soren Bale. | SRC-003 | Apprenticeship register. |
| 1970 | 21 | Completes apprenticeship. Bale's final assessment praises precision but notes that Elias avoids open disagreement. | SRC-004 | Written assessment summary; no exact quotation approved. |
| 1972 | 23 | Becomes junior foreman on the Old Mill conversion. Stops a wall pour after noticing misplaced reinforcement, his first recorded site intervention. | SRC-005 | Site diary and revised schedule. |
| 1974 | 25 | Marries Ana Rell. Ana later manages accounts and contracts for the business. | SRC-006 | Civil and business records. |
| 1976 | 27 | Establishes Vale Works with Ana and two carpenters. | SRC-007 | Registration and first ledger. |
| 1981 | 32 | Completes River Market Arcade, bringing regional attention and larger commissions. | SRC-008 | Project archive and trade review. |
| 1986 | 37 | Wins contract for North Quay School annex under a compressed public timetable. | SRC-009 | Contract file. |
| 1987 | 38 | Approves a substitute batch of roof timber and a shortened inspection sequence after repeated schedule pressure. During a winter storm, roof trusses fail overnight. No one is injured; the building is closed and twelve classrooms are unusable. | SRC-010, SRC-011 | Multiple contributing causes; approval is decisive but not sole cause. |
| 1988 | 39 | Inquiry identifies inadequate timber grading, altered connections, insufficient inspection, and management pressure. Elias accepts professional responsibility for signing the change. Licence suspended for eighteen months. | SRC-011, SRC-012 | Preserve shared causation. |
| 1988-1989 | 39-40 | Works as a site carpenter outside regulated supervisory duties, studies structural inspection, and assists with restitution payments administered by Ana. | SRC-013, SRC-014 | Not exile or destitution; finances are strained but stable. |
| 1990 | 41 | Licence restored after review. Elias introduces the “three proofs” rule: approved drawing, verified material, witnessed inspection. | SRC-015 | Rule documented in company manual. |
| 1993 | 44 | Completes Cedar Court, a small housing project noted for clear inspection records and resident involvement. | SRC-016 | Municipal archive and resident committee notes. |
| 1998 | 49 | Begins restoration practice, arguing that repair requires understanding why earlier work survived or failed. | SRC-017 | Lecture notes and project list. |
| 2004 | 55 | Leads restoration of Guild Hall after rejecting a cheaper method that would have concealed moisture damage. | SRC-018 | Tender comparison and conservation report. |
| 2007 | 58 | Declines a profitable tower contract because foundation information is incomplete. The client hires another firm; later redesign confirms deeper piling was required. | SRC-019 | Correspondence and planning revision. |
| 2010 | 61 | Takes Mara Senn as an apprentice after formal apprenticeship programmes in the region have declined. | SRC-020 | Agreement and training notebook. |
| 2016 | 67 | Ana dies after a short illness. Elias reduces commercial work and begins teaching weekly at Stonewick Craft School. | SRC-021 | School record and family notice. |
| 2018 | 69 | Begins Lantern Hall with Mara as project architect-builder. Elias acts as senior builder and mentor rather than sole author. | SRC-022 | Contract and role statement. |
| 2020 | 71 | Lantern Hall opens as a public library, repair workshop, and apprenticeship classroom. | SRC-023 | Completion record and programme. |
| 2021 | 72 | Donates notebooks, inspection templates, and project records to Stonewick Craft School. | SRC-024 | Archive inventory. |
| 2023 | 74 | Dies at home. Mara delivers the memorial address; no text is supplied in the dossier and none may be invented. | SRC-025 | Death notice and programme only. |
