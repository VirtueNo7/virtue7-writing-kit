# Project Records

## PRJ-001 - Old Mill Conversion, 1972

**Role:** Junior foreman.  
**Narrative purpose:** First documented occasion when Elias stops work despite schedule pressure.  
**Fact:** Reinforcement had been placed incorrectly before a wall pour.  
**Boundary:** The dossier contains no dramatic argument and no claim that collapse was imminent.

## PRJ-002 - River Market Arcade, 1981

**Role:** Lead builder.  
**Narrative purpose:** Establishes reputation and exposes the transition from small craft work to larger contractual systems.  
**Fact:** Completed within agreed cost after a six-week delay caused by river flooding.  
**Interpretive possibility:** Success increased confidence but also placed Elias in situations where paperwork, authority, and institutional pressure mattered more than before.

## PRJ-003 - North Quay School Annex, 1986-1988

**Role:** Principal contractor and signing supervisor.  
**Narrative purpose:** Defining professional failure.  
**Facts:** Schedule compressed; timber substitution approved; inspection shortened; trusses failed overnight during a winter storm; no injuries; inquiry found multiple causes; Elias's signed approval was decisive; licence suspended eighteen months.  
**Boundary:** Do not imply criminal conviction, deaths, bribery, deliberate concealment, or sole causation.

## PRJ-004 - Cedar Court, 1993

**Role:** Principal builder.  
**Narrative purpose:** Shows changed process through ordinary competent work rather than instant heroic redemption.  
**Facts:** Twelve homes, resident committee review, visible inspection schedule, modest commercial result, no award.

## PRJ-005 - Guild Hall Restoration, 2004

**Role:** Restoration lead.  
**Narrative purpose:** Demonstrates mature judgement and willingness to defend necessary work.  
**Facts:** Elias rejected a cheap surface repair; opening revealed concealed moisture damage; final project cost increased by fourteen percent and prevented further decay.  
**Boundary:** The dossier does not claim he alone discovered the damage.

## PRJ-006 - East Bank Tower Proposal, 2007

**Role:** Declined bidder.  
**Narrative purpose:** Provides contrast with North Quay.  
**Facts:** Elias refused to price the work as fixed-scope without further foundation investigation; another team was appointed; later redesign required deeper piling.  
**Boundary:** Do not claim a disaster was narrowly avoided.

## PRJ-007 - Lantern Hall, 2018-2020

**Role:** Senior builder and mentor; Mara Senn held design leadership.  
**Narrative purpose:** Final synthesis of craft, documentation, accessibility, teaching, and succession.  
**Facts:** Public library, repair workshop, and apprenticeship classroom; reused stone from a demolished warehouse; opened in 2020.  
**Boundary:** Do not frame Mara as merely completing Elias's vision.
