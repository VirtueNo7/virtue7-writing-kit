# Master Builder Biography Demonstration

This demonstration contains a complete fictional dossier. It is designed to test the writing kit without external research or user-supplied information.

## Subject

**Elias Vale (1949-2023)** - fictional master builder, contractor, teacher, and restorer.

## Test objective

Create an expandable and collapsible biography that shows how technical skill, judgement, responsibility, failure, correction, and teaching developed across one working life.

## Restrictions

- Every person, place, institution, project, and source is fictional.
- Do not browse for the subject.
- Do not imply that the dossier describes the uploader.
- Do not invent events, dialogue, quotations, awards, or motives beyond the dossier.
- Do not make Elias a flawless hero or reduce his life to the 1987 failure.
- Preserve shared causation: the failure had several contributing factors, while Elias's signed approval remains a decisive act for which he accepted responsibility.
- Keep technical explanations intelligible to a general reader.
- The generated biography must not contain Virtue7 branding unless the user requests a branded demonstration.

## Recommended representative unit

The 1987 North Quay School annex failure and the period immediately following it. This unit tests chronology, technical explanation, responsibility, consequence, interpretation, and later change.
